Our Method

How Dynamic Traders invest

We combine rigorous fundamental analysis with disciplined technical observation to find and manage positions with a genuine edge — without the noise.

01

Fundamental Screening

We identify companies with strong, improving fundamentals — businesses worth owning at the right price. Quality first, always.

02

Technical Entry Timing

Price action and trend analysis tell us when the market agrees with our thesis. We wait for confirmation before committing capital.

03

Dynamic Position Management

We compound into winning positions and size down or exit when conditions change. Patience is our greatest edge.

04

Risk-First Discipline

Every position is sized relative to portfolio risk, not conviction alone. Protecting capital is the prerequisite to growing it.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are text files containing small amounts of information which are downloaded to your device when you visit a website. Cookies are then sent back to the originating website on each subsequent visit, or to another website that recognises that cookie. Cookies allow our systems to recognise your device and make any presettings immediately available.

Why Are Cookies Useful?

  • They allow the Dynamic Trader website to recognise your device and target content to your interests
  • They help us to improve our website and offer you a better, more tailored service
  • They enable us to recognise your device when you return to our website

By using our website you agree that, unless you have set your browser to reject them, we can place cookies on your device and use that data in accordance with this policy.

Types of Cookies

First party cookies are served directly by us to your computer and are used to recognise your computer when it revisits our site and to remember your preferences as you browse.

Third party cookies are served by a service provider on our behalf and can be used by the service provider to recognise your computer when you visit other websites. Third party cookies are most commonly used for website analytics or advertising purposes.

Changing Your Cookie Preferences

If you do not want to use cookies, you can set your browser so that the storage of cookies is not accepted. If you only want to accept our own cookies but not those of our service providers and partners, select the "block third-party cookies" setting in your browser. Most web browsers allow some control over cookies through browser settings. Find out more at allaboutcookies.org or youronlinechoices.eu.
